About me

Passionate UX designer who transforms complex problems into elegant, human-centered solutions. By creating intuitive and visually appealing experiences that engage and inspire.

What I do

UX design, prototyping, user testing and research. I'm passionate about creating user-friendly and engaging digital products.

How I work

My design process centers on empathy, creative problem solving, and collaboration. Rooted in user centered design principles, I start by deeply understanding user needs, then work iteratively with cross-functional teams and real users to test, refine, and validate solutions until they genuinely solve the right problems.
