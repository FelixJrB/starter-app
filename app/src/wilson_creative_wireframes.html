Wireframe design for website improvements

During my internship at Wilson Creative, I had the opportunity to develop wireframes for two different client projects, where I focused on improving the user experience through layout restructuring, SEO optimization, and increased website relevance.

Bluepartner - Consulting company in Kalmar

For Bluepartner, I carried out an extensive restructuring of their website with a focus on:

  • Content clarification - Restructured the information for better readability and clarity
  • Navigation Optimization - Simplified user flows and created a more intuitive website structure
  • SEO improvements - Implemented strategies for better searchability rankings through consulting with the team
  • Visual modernization - Updated the design language for a more contemporary and professional look

Hagblom - Group company southern/central Sweden

The project with Hagblom required a methodical approach where I:

  • Analyzed existing structure - Mapped current website architecture and identified bottlenecks
  • Identified usability issues - Conducted user experience evaluation
  • Developed impact map - Created a visual overview of where to focus the resources and to gain further understanding of the implementation and the expected improvements and their impact on business goals
  • Developed improvement proposals - Presented concrete solutions based on discovered problems
  • Implemented the changes iteratively - Worked closely with the development team for gradual improvement

Lessons learned and development

These projects strengthened my skills in:

  • User-centered design process
  • Understanding of business needs and goals
  • Clear communication from concept to finished solution
  • Collaboration with development teams in iterative processes

By working with two different types of companies – from a local consulting firm to a larger corporation – I gained valuable experience in how UX design needs to be adapted to different business contexts and target groups.
